private subroutine set_scale_factor()

The scale factor to switch between Cartesian and cylindrical geometries is set here, along with its derivative. For cylindrical the scale factor is simply equal to the Gaussian grid, and its derivative is unity. For Cartesian the scale factor is unity and its derivative is zero.

private subroutine accumulate_mesh_special_fluxtube()

Special mesh accumulation routine for flux tubes. Instead of a Gaussian a 60-30-10 percentage-distribution is used, meaning that 60% of the gridpoints are located between the axis and , 30% of the gridpoints are located at a centered at the inner fluxtube radius , and the remaining 10% is located in the outside region.

private subroutine accumulate_mesh()

Re-grids the mesh to a non-uniform spacing using mesh accumulation. This is based on two Gaussian curves with known widths, the grid is accumulated near each Gaussian maximum using equidistribution based on the integral under the curve defined by the function gaussian().
